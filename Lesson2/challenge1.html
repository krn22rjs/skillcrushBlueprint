My Favorite Tips for Freelances

Becoming a freelance was one of the best career decisions I've ever made. So I want to share some of the most important things I learned
to help you succeed as a freelancer.

Project Management

To succeed as a freelancer, you need to be organized when it comes to your work. Whether you're working on a solo project or as part of
larger team, you need to know how to manage your time and resources effectively.

Promotion

As a freelancer, you need to get your name out there so potential clients can find you. Promoting yourself online these days is a snap!

Social Media

One of the best ways to promote your services is through social media. Be sure to use a scheduling tool to save yourself some time and
effort.
