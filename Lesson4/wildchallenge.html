  1. Bravery Award: for just getting started!

  4. Master List Maker Award: ordered or unordered list with at least one list
  5. Head Award: use of a tag we haven't covered yet
  6. Autobiographer Award: some text about yourself (hint: including where you were born!)
  7. Artist Award: image tag with src attribute
  8. Polyglot Awards: script tag
  9. Connector Award: hyperlink
